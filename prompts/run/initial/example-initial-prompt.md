## Instructions

Read the research brief in `context/from-human/research-brief.md` and all other context files. Then conduct an initial survey of the topic.

Your output should include:

1. **Landscape overview** — What are the major dimensions of this topic?
2. **Key players / sources** — Who are the authoritative voices? What are the primary sources?
3. **Current state** — What is the established consensus (if any)?
4. **Open questions** — What remains debated, uncertain, or under-researched?
5. **Suggested deep-dive areas** — Based on the research brief, which areas warrant follow-up prompts?

Save the output to `outputs/individual/` with today's date and a descriptive slug.
