## Steps

1. Read the research brief from `context/from-human/research-brief.md` and report the topic.
2. Count and list:
   - Prompts in `prompts/queue/` (pending)
   - Prompts in `prompts/run/initial/` (completed initial)
   - Prompts in `prompts/run/subsequent/` (completed follow-up)
   - Prompts in `prompts/drafting/` (in development)
3. Count and list outputs in `outputs/individual/` with dates.
4. Check for compaction files in `context/from-history/` and report the most recent.
5. Check for aggregated reports in `outputs/aggregated/`.
6. Check for final deliverables in `outputs/final/`.
7. Present a concise status summary in table format.
